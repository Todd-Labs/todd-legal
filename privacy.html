Privacy Policy

Effective as of July 23, 2026

About

This privacy policy applies to the Todd app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Todd Labs (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

Todd does not require an account or email address. You may choose to provide a name, fitness preferences, workout logs, goals, notes, edits, AI chat messages, and issue reports. Most of this information is stored only on your device.

When you use an AI assisted or voice feature, Todd sends the information needed to complete that request. Depending on the feature, this may include typed or spoken text, voice audio or transcripts, workout and goal details, notes, edit instructions, recent AI conversation context, and a limited set of relevant workout history and calculated goal progress for goal analysis. Todd uses this information to transcribe, format, edit, analyze, or troubleshoot the content you requested and, as described below, to operate usage limits, secure the service, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

To operate and protect the service, Todd also processes a randomly generated installation identifier, request timestamps and types, success or failure status, usage and cost information, error details, model responses, final structured results, and purchase or subscription status. The installation identifier is hashed before it is stored in the backend and is not your name or email address. Network and service providers may also process your Internet Protocol address and basic device or connection information when requests are transmitted.

Todd does not collect precise location, track you across other companies' apps or websites, sell personal information, or use your information for advertising or marketing.

Local Data Storage

Your workout logs, goals, profile information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They leave your device only when you choose a feature that needs remote processing, such as AI assisted logging or editing, voice transcription, goal analysis, subscription verification, or issue reporting. Temporary audio files created on your device are deleted after the transcription request completes. Todd does not store raw voice audio in its own backend, but transcripts and related request records may be retained as described below. If you submit an issue report, the report text and a copy of the related workout or goal are sent to Todd's secure backend so the problem can be investigated.

Microphone and Voice Data

The Application requests microphone and speech recognition access only to provide voice input. When you use voice for workout logging or editing, goal creation or editing, or workout note creation or editing, audio may be transmitted securely to Deepgram for speech to text transcription. Apple's Speech framework may also process voice input as a fallback; Todd does not guarantee that Apple Speech processing occurs only on your device. Voice audio and transcripts are used to provide transcription, improve transcription reliability, diagnose failures, and protect the service, not for advertising.

Push Notifications

The Application may send push notifications to remind you of workout goals, celebrate achievements, and provide progress updates based on your activity. You can disable notifications at any time through your iPhone Settings.

Third Party Access

Todd transmits user provided content to service providers only when needed for a feature you choose, as described in the Third Party Services section below. Todd requires these providers to protect the data consistently with this policy. Their processing is governed by their service terms, privacy commitments, and Todd's account settings.

The Service Provider may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • When they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • With trusted service providers that process information on Todd's behalf for the purposes described in this policy.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI — Todd's active AI features use OpenAI models through a secure Supabase proxy. Depending on the feature, OpenAI may receive workout and goal descriptions, voice transcripts, notes, edit instructions, recent AI conversation context, relevant workout history, and calculated goal progress to format, edit, or analyze the user's requested content. OpenAI handles API data under its published API data controls and retention practices.
  • Apple Speech Framework — Apple Speech may process voice input as a fallback when transcription is unavailable or incomplete. Processing may occur on device or through Apple services depending on device capabilities and settings.
  • Deepgram — Voice audio for workout logs and edits, goal creation and edits, and workout note creation and edits may be securely transmitted to Deepgram for speech to text transcription and transcription reliability. Deepgram handles audio under its customer data terms, privacy policy, and Todd's Deepgram account settings.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription billing and purchase verification is managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives purchase history and subscription status information, which Todd and RevenueCat use to verify access, prevent fraud, and provide subscription analytics.
  • Supabase — Provides the secure API proxy and backend records used to route AI and transcription requests, enforce usage limits, investigate errors, improve quality, and prevent abuse. Records may include submitted text, transcripts, model responses, final structured results, timestamps, request type, technical metadata, and a hashed installation identifier.

Subscription and Billing

The Application offers auto-renewing subscriptions processed through Apple's App Store. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription through your Apple ID Account Settings. Refund requests and eligibility are determined by Apple under its policies.

Opt-Out Rights

You can avoid future AI or voice processing by not using those features, revoke microphone or speech recognition access in iPhone Settings, or stop all future use by uninstalling the Application. Disabling these permissions will prevent the related voice features from working. Uninstalling the Application removes its local data but does not automatically delete records already processed by Todd's backend or third party providers.

Data Retention Policy

Local workout, goal, profile, note, and preference data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Application. Temporary on-device voice recordings are deleted after the transcription request completes. Backend request records, including submitted text, transcripts, model responses, final results, and technical metadata, are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate usage limits, diagnose and correct problems, improve service quality, prevent abuse, resolve issue reports, and meet legal obligations. Third party providers retain information according to their own policies and Todd's account settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete local data by deleting entries in the Application or uninstalling it. To request deletion of backend records that Todd can reasonably identify, contact toddlabs413@gmail.com. Because Todd does not require an account and backend records use a hashed installation identifier rather than your name or email, Todd may need additional details about the approximate date and type of your requests to locate the correct records. Todd will respond within a reasonable time and delete identifiable data as required by applicable law.

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information the Service Provider processes and maintains.

You can withdraw future microphone and speech access in iPhone Settings or stop using the AI and voice features, but withdrawing consent does not undo processing that already occurred.
