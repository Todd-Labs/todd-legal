About

This privacy policy applies to the Todd app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Todd Labs (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to enhance user experience and provide certain features. The AI components may process user data to deliver personalized content, recommendations, or automated functionalities. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws. If you have questions about the AI features or data processing, please contact the Service Provider.

The Service Provider may use the information you provided to contact you from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to First Name, Email, Usage Data, Fitness/Health Data. The information that the Service Provider request will be retained by them and used as described in this privacy policy.

Local Data Storage

Your workout logs, goals, profile information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any remote server except when workout descriptions, goal entries, voice transcripts, or temporary voice audio are processed by trusted AI services for formatting and transcription. When those requests are processed, the text you submit and the AI's response may be retained in our secure backend for diagnostics, quality, and abuse prevention, associated with a randomly generated device identifier rather than your name.

Microphone and Voice Data

The Application requests access to your device microphone solely to enable voice based workout logging. To convert your speech into a workout log, your voice audio is typically transmitted securely to a trusted third party transcription service (Deepgram); on device transcription using Apple's Speech Framework is used as a fallback. Voice audio is used only for transcription and diagnostic comparison, not advertising.

Push Notifications

The Application may send push notifications to remind you of workout goals, celebrate achievements, and provide progress updates based on your activity. You can disable notifications at any time through your iPhone Settings.

Third Party Access

Certain user provided content, such as workout descriptions and goal entries, may be transmitted to trusted third party service providers as described in the Third Party Services section below. The Service Provider may share your information with third parties in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.

The Service Provider may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • When they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • With their trusted services providers who work on their behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI (openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) and Anthropic (anthropic.com/privacy) — User workout descriptions and goal entries are sent via a secure Supabase proxy to third party AI models — primarily OpenAI, with Anthropic's Claude as a fallback — to format and structure entries. Text submitted through these APIs is not used to train the providers' models and is retained only briefly, if at all, for abuse monitoring.
  • Apple Speech Framework (apple.com/legal/privacy) — Voice input for workout logging may be transcribed locally on the user's device using Apple's built in Speech Framework.
  • Deepgram (deepgram.com/privacy) — Voice audio may be securely transmitted to Deepgram for speech to text transcription and transcription quality comparison.
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com/privacy) — Subscription billing and purchase verification is managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives purchase confirmation and subscription status information.
  • Supabase (supabase.com/privacy) — Provides the secure API proxy that routes AI transcription and formatting requests. For diagnostics, quality, and abuse prevention, the text of your requests and the AI's responses may be logged here, associated with a randomly generated device identifier.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription through your Apple ID Account Settings. No refunds will be provided for partial subscription periods. All refund requests are handled by Apple.
