Features

Everything, on the books.

Todd is a simple guy. You do the work, he does the paperwork.

🎙️

Log by voice or keyboard

Speak or type your workout in plain English. Todd handles the numbers — sets, reps, weight, rest, time, distance, and steps.

🧾

AI formatting

Messy gym-speak in, clean structured logs out. Every workout is organized by exercise with tidy rows you can trust.

🎯

Goals Todd can measure

A 225 bench, a sub-5 mile, 100,000 steps this month, or "100 push-ups a day for 30 days" — with a streak calendar that fills in one green circle per day.

Streaks, total volume, estimated one-rep maxes, a consistency heatmap, and per-exercise charts — including steps.
